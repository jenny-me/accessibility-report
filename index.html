Report Overview

WCAG guidelines are divided into four principles, briefly describing the goal of the guidelines. The guidelines are further divided into three levels that indicate the priority of conformance. Level A is the highest priority and Level AAA is lowest priority. This report targets the mid-range Level AA conformance.
